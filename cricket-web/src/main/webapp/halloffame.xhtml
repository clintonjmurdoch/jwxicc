Hall of Fame - Johnnie Walker XI

The Johnnie Walker XI Hall of Fame honours those players who have made a significant contribution to the Johnnie Walker XI. To earn selection, one must achieve at least one of the following criterion:

  • Club founder
  • Club captain
  • Century in an innings
  • Five wickets in an innings
  • 100 matches
  • 2000 career runs
  • 100 wickets
  • Any other achievement deemed worthy of selection
